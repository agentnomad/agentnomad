About Us

Agent Nomad is your go-to travel planning platform, designed for travelers who love to take control of their own itineraries. Our site offers a blend of intuitive tools and AI-powered suggestions to create unique, personalized trips.

What Makes Us Different

  • **Custom Itineraries**: View your plans in both list and map formats, ensuring you can visualize your trip your way.
  • **AI Assistance**: Use our smart recommendations to find nearby attractions without following cookie-cutter itineraries.
  • **Day Grouping**: Organize must-see destinations and discover complementary activities to group into day plans.
  • **Backup Plans**: Add "if there's time" locations for flexibility, so you're always prepared.
  • **Export Options**: Export your finalized itineraries to an Excel sheet for easy access and sharing.

Plan Your Way

Whether you're a meticulous planner or prefer to go with the flow, Agent Nomad gives you the tools to create trips that reflect your unique style. From choosing must-see destinations to planning spontaneous side trips, we make travel planning enjoyable and stress-free.
