About Agent Nomad

Agent Nomad is your intuitive travel planning platform designed for self-planners who seek a personalized and dynamic approach to organizing their trips. Our site allows you to:

  • View your itinerary on a map or as a list.
  • Use AI to guide your planning without providing generic itineraries.
  • Select must-see places and discover nearby attractions to group into daily plans.
  • Add backup places for "if there's time" moments.

Intelligent Features

Our AI helps you build unique itineraries by grouping attractions based on proximity, duration, and your interests, ensuring every day is optimized for maximum enjoyment. Say goodbye to generic plans and hello to your custom adventure.

Tailored for You

At Agent Nomad, we believe travel should be as unique as you are. With tools for prioritizing your must-see spots and flexible backups, our planner adapts to your preferences and plans.
